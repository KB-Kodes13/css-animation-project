5 Best Fall Jackets for Men 2023

Leather

The rugged look and timeless appeal of leather will continue to be a trend winter after winter.

Denim

Denim is an all time great fabric. Durable and lots of texture whether you choose jean, shirt, or jacket form you can't go wrong.

Parka

Whether in a mod-inspired fishtail style or something more suited to an arctic exploration, parkas are big, bold and can be thrown over virtually anything.

Varsity Jackets

Varsity jackets have been cool for going-on a century. Short in the body and with ribbed hems and cuffs, they have always worked as part of a preppy look.

Honorable Mention: Oversized Cardigans

With dropped shoulders and a slightly longer length, you can treat this style of cardigan as a replacement for a lightweight jacket, perfect for keeping warm on those cool Fall evenings.
